# Earning the IBM Generative AI Fundamentals Certificate

I recently completed the **Getting Started with Generative AI** course through IBM SkillsBuild, earning a certificate that deepened my understanding of artificial intelligence and its practical applications in software development.

![IBM Generative AI Certificate](/images/blog/getting-started-with-generative-ai.png)

## What I Learned

The course provided a comprehensive introduction to generative AI fundamentals, covering:

### Core Concepts
- **Foundations of AI and Machine Learning**: Understanding the building blocks that power modern AI systems
- **Large Language Models (LLMs)**: How models like GPT and Claude work under the hood
- **Generative AI Applications**: Real-world use cases across industries—from content creation to code generation
- **Prompt Engineering**: Crafting effective prompts to get better results from AI tools
- **Ethical Considerations**: Understanding bias, responsible AI usage, and limitations

### Practical Applications
The course emphasized hands-on understanding of how generative AI can be applied in software development:
- Code generation and assistance
- Documentation and technical writing
- Problem-solving and debugging support
- Workflow automation

## Why This Matters

As a developer, understanding generative AI isn't just about using tools like ChatGPT or Claude Code—it's about knowing **how** and **when** to apply them effectively. During my internship at Endoo, I worked extensively with AI technologies, integrating AWS Bedrock (Claude) for automated translations and content generation in the Odoo module I built.

This certificate validates that foundation and equips me to:
- Make informed decisions about AI tool integration
- Understand the capabilities and limitations of generative AI
- Apply AI responsibly in production environments
- Stay current with rapidly evolving AI technologies

## Moving Forward

Generative AI is transforming how we build software, and this is just the beginning. I'm excited to continue exploring advanced AI applications, particularly in:
- AI-powered developer tools and workflows
- Intelligent automation in enterprise systems
- Natural language interfaces for complex applications

The future of software development is increasingly collaborative—humans and AI working together to solve problems faster and more creatively. This certificate is another step in that journey.
